Apollo Lake: Overview - Intel
The Intel Atom® processor E3900 series, Intel Celeron processor N3350,
and Intel Pentium processor N4200 platform, formerly Apollo Lake,
empowers real-time computing in digital surveillance, new in-vehicle
experiences, advancements in industrial and office automation, new 
solutions for retail and medical, and more. These processors are based
on the Goldmont architecture, utilizing Intel’s industry-leading
14 nm process technology.
